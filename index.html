What is Karo Pitch?

Thousands of founders are building amazing businesses but lack access to investors. Karo Pitch connects founders with investors through curated pitch events and a discovery platform.

Our mission is to support founders across Bharat, especially in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities, giving them the stage they deserve to showcase their vision to the world.

  • Curated Pitch Events
  • Direct Investor Access
  • Mentorship & Guidance

How It Works

Your journey from application to funding in 4 simple steps.

01

Apply with Pitch Deck

Submit your startup details, traction, and pitch deck through our simple application portal.

02

Get Shortlisted

KaroStartup's expert panel reviews your application and shortlists the most promising startups.

03

Pitch Live

Present your vision live to a curated panel of top Indian investors, VCs, and angel networks.

04

Raise & Scale

Secure funding, get strategic guidance, and take your startup to the next level of growth.
